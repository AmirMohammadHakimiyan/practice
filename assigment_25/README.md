|  *COUNTRY* | *TIME* |
| - | -------------- |

| Iran | 07:01:00 |

## alarm
1. To **edit** the alarm , enter the alarm manually then 
click the button.
2. To **add** new alarm click on "+".
3. To **remove** alarm click on "-".
